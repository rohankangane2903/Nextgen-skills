Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a course?

You can apply for any of our courses by filling out the application form on our website or by visiting our campus. Our admissions team will guide you through the process and help you choose the right program based on your background and career goals.

What are the payment options available?

We offer multiple payment options including full payment, installment plans, and education loans through our banking partners. You can pay via credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, or demand draft.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, we have a dedicated placement cell that works with our industry partners to help students find suitable job opportunities. We provide career counseling, resume building, interview preparation, and organize campus recruitment drives.

Can I visit the campus before enrolling?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective students to visit our campus, meet our faculty, and see our facilities. You can schedule a campus tour by filling out the contact form or calling our admissions office.
